Don't Fuss Organizing Your Next Fantasy Draft

EasyDraft is a tool that allows you to easily perform fantasy football drafts with friends by keeping track of picks, displaying statistics, and more. Therefore dont fuss with pencil and paper on your next draft day. Use our big board and have a draft both worth remembering and a setup thats a breeze.

How to Use EasyDraft

In order to use EasyDraft, you should create an EasyDraft account. Once you have an account you can start a new league, setup your draft, and invite your friends over for draft day. You can view all of your current teams at any time to compare previous drafts and prepare for your next draft day victory.
